Siva is a Developer Advocate at JetBrains with over 19 years of experience building scalable, distributed enterprise systems. His career spans hands-on software development, architecture, and cloud-native system design across domains such as banking and e-commerce.

He has deep expertise in the Java ecosystem and modern backend development using monolithic, microservices, and event-driven architectures, with strong emphasis on code quality and scalability.

In his current role as a Developer Advocate, Siva focuses on empowering developers by simplifying complex concepts, creating educational content, and bridging the gap between engineering and developer communities. He actively shares practical insights through his blog (https://sivalabs.in) and YouTube channel (https://youtube.com/sivalabs).

Siva is the author of Beginning Spring Boot (Apress), PrimeFaces Beginners Guide (PacktPub), and Java Persistence with MyBatis 3. He has spoken at international and local conferences, sharing insights on cloud-native architectures, modern Java development, and developer productivity.
